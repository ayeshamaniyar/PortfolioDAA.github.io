Phase 1: City Map & Topography

Spanning an area of 1500 sq km, Indraprastha is a city located near New Delhi, India, within the district of "Pratigna" and having the pincode 110002. The city's topography is predominantly a flat terrain, situated on the fertile Indo-Gangetic plain.
